Awsum Pawsum

Awsum Pawsum lets the player take control of a revenge seeking hero. After nearly being hit by a drunk driver out protagonist finds themselves fighting off snakes and frogs to reach the big city. Waiting there for him, lies his true enemy.

Awsum Pawsum was the February Spartasoft Game Jam from Team Birdo. This time around I brought 8 other people to join me on this project. I was the producer and designer for this project. I utilized google docs to create and effective way to communicate what sections of the game needed to be worked on. While working on this project I got a handle on what everyone was capable of doing, this let me effectively know the scope of what we could achieve in 48 hours. While doing this project multiple different ideas were thrown together to create a small gameplay loop. This game jam game helped me a lot with getting experience in producing.
