Awsum Pawsum

Beware the wrath of the nearly run over. Roadkill strikes back against the reckless drivers of the night in this beat-em-up adventure. Filled with intoxicatingly entertaining designs and music, get ready to enter the twisted world and mind of the Awsome Pawsum!

Awsum Pawsum was the February Spartasoft Game Jam from Team Birdo. This time around I brought 8 other people to join me on this project. I was the producer and designer for this project. I utilized google docs to create and effective way to communicate what sections of the game needed to be worked on. While working on this project I got a handle on what everyone was capable of doing, this let me effectively know the scope of what we could achieve in 48 hours. While doing this project multiple different ideas were thrown together to create a small gameplay loop. The game

has amazing art, music and sound effects. This game jam game helped me a lot with getting experience into producing.
