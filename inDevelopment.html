Mission Start:Eternal Sunshine Laboratories

Mission Start:Eternal Sunshine Laboratories is a 2D rouge-like is currently in development. This game focuses more on having a fun story and light hearted humor. The game will release initially with 3 levels, 25 items, 10 enemies, 3 bosses, and 5 NPCs. In this game you play as Sol, a worker for the NHCC(National Hazard Control Center). Working with him is Dr.J, a goofy but ecentric partner who helps via remote communication. The projected release date for this game is March 2023.
